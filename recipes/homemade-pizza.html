Homemade Pizza

Oven baked Homemade Pizza with pepperoni and cheese

Ingridients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Tomato sauce (smooth or pureed)
  • Fresh soft mozzarella cheese, separated into small clumps
  • Italian pepperoncini, thinly sliced

Method

Preparing the dough
  1. Proof yeast: mix water and yeast in bowl and let sit for 5 minutes
  2. Add flour, salt, sugar and olive oil. Knead dough for 7-10 minutes
  3. Let the dough rise: cover with plastic wrap for 90 minutes
Preparing the Pizzas
  1. Preheat the pizza stone: heat oven to 475 F anf place stone inside
  2. Divide dough into 2 balls, place into separate bowls and cover with plastic wrap for 15 minutes
  3. Prep the toppings
  4. Flatten dough ball, and stretch out into a round
  5. Brush dough with olive oil and add tomato sauce and toppings
  6. Slide pizza into oven on pizza stone and bake at 475 F for 10-15 minutes

Enjoy!
