Old Fashioned

Traditional Old Fashioned whiskey Cocktail

Ingridients

  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon water
  • 2 dashes bitters
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger bourbon whiskey
  • 1 slice orange
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Method

  1. Pour simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whiskey glass; stir to combine.
  2. Add ice cubes and pour in bourbon.
  3. Garnish with orange slice and maraschino cherry.

Enjoy!
